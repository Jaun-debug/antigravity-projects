Frequently Asked Questions About Namibia Luxury Safaris

What defines a luxury safari in Namibia?

A luxury safari in Namibia is all about exclusivity, comfort, and personalised service. Expect high-end lodges in remote locations, exceptional guiding, gourmet dining, and carefully curated experiences in nature.

When is the best time to travel?

Namibia is a year-round destination, but the dry season (May to October) is ideal for wildlife viewing, especially in Etosha National Park. The green season (November to April) offers dramatic landscapes and fewer travellers.

How do you travel between destinations?

Luxury safaris often include light aircraft flights between lodges, reducing long driving times and offering spectacular aerial views of areas like Sossusvlei and the Skeleton Coast.

What type of accommodation can I expect?

You can expect boutique lodges and luxury camps with spacious suites, private decks, plunge pools, and stunning views — often located in exclusive concessions or private reserves.

Is Namibia suitable for a luxury safari compared to other African destinations?

Absolutely. Namibia stands out for its vast open spaces, low visitor density, and unique desert landscapes, offering a more private and tranquil luxury experience compared to busier safari destinations.

Are luxury safaris suitable for families?

Yes, many lodges in Namibia welcome families and offer tailored activities for children, although some properties have age restrictions due to their remote and tranquil setting.

What wildlife can I expect on a luxury safari?

Wildlife viewing is excellent, particularly in Etosha National Park, where you may see elephants, lions, rhinos, giraffes, and a variety of plains game, often from exclusive viewpoints or guided experiences.
