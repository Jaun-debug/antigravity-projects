Day-by-Day Itinerary

01
Kalahari Desert — Intu Afrika Kalahari Game Reserve

Day 1 - 3

Your Namibia self-drive safari begins as you arrive and head into the iconic Kalahari Desert, where rolling red dunes and open savannah landscapes set the tone for your journey. Settle into your lodge and ease into the rhythm of the desert.

Over the next two days, explore this vast reserve known for meerkats, oryx, springbok, and giraffe, while enjoying optional guided Bushman walks, quad biking, and nature drives. Evenings are best spent under endless African skies, with classic sundowners and stargazing experiences.

02
Fish River Canyon

Day 3 - 5

Travel south to one of Namibia’s most dramatic natural landmarks, the Fish River Canyon, the second-largest canyon in the world. The drive itself showcases remote desert scenery and wide open landscapes.

Spend your time exploring scenic viewpoints such as Hobas, taking in panoramic canyon views, or relaxing at the nearby Ai-Ais hot springs. For active travellers, optional hiking trails, scenic flights, and nature drives offer deeper exploration of this rugged region.

03
Lüderitz & Kolmanskop

Day 5 - 7

Continue to the coastal town of Lüderitz, where German colonial charm meets the wild Atlantic coastline. The colourful buildings, Art Nouveau architecture, and ocean views create a unique atmosphere unlike anywhere else in Namibia.

A highlight here is visiting Kolmanskop Ghost Town, where abandoned buildings are slowly being reclaimed by desert sand—perfect for photography and history lovers. Along the coast, spot flamingos, seals, and penguins, or simply enjoy the quiet coastal setting.

04
Namib Desert

Day 7 - 8

Your journey leads back inland into the vast Namib Desert, one of the oldest deserts in the world. This region offers raw, untouched landscapes with striking contrasts between dunes, mountains, and open plains.

Enjoy a peaceful overnight stay surrounded by desert silence, with opportunities for stargazing, nature walks, and scenic drives through this remote and timeless environment.

05
Sossusvlei & Namib-Naukluft Park

Day 8 - 10

Arrive in the world-famous Sossusvlei, home to Namibia’s iconic red dunes and one of the country’s top safari highlights. Early mornings are ideal for visiting Deadvlei, climbing Big Daddy dune, and capturing the dramatic desert light.

This region is rich in desert-adapted wildlife and offers optional activities such as hot air balloon safaris, scenic flights, and guided excursions. The combination of towering dunes, white clay pans, and endless desert views creates a truly unforgettable experience.

06
Windhoek

Day 10 - 11

Return to Windhoek, Namibia’s capital city, for your final evening. After days in remote wilderness, enjoy the comfort of the city with its mix of modern amenities, German-influenced culture, and excellent restaurants.

This is the perfect time to unwind, reflect on your journey, and enjoy a final dinner—recommended at well-known spots like Stellenbosch Wine Bar or Butcher’s Block.
