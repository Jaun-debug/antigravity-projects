Welcome to Beach Lodge

Beach Lodge is one of the few accommodation establishments in Swakopmund that are situated right on the beach and in fact you will not be able to get any closer to the sea than this. Enjoy the magnificent view of the sea from each room, listen to the restless waves and smell the fresh sea breeze.

We offer accommodation in standard family rooms, twin rooms and luxury rooms in different configurations, creating the ideal home away from home for the individual traveller, families and corporate travellers alike.
