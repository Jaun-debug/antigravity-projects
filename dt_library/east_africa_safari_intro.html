An East Africa safari through Tanzania and Kenya, combined with the tropical beaches of Zanzibar, offers one of the most iconic safari experiences in the world. From the vast plains of the Serengeti National Park to the legendary Maasai Mara, this is where Africa’s great wildlife spectacles unfold.

Paired with the white sandy beaches and turquoise waters of Zanzibar, it’s the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation — a journey that captures the very essence of Africa.
