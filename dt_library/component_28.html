Fish River Canyon — Fish River Lodge

Day 3 - 4

Day 3 - 4 is yours to spend at Fish River Lodge, a place where the waterways and their wildlife set the agenda. Mornings on the river — by boat or mokoro — offer close encounters with hippo, elephant, and a remarkable diversity of birds. The water catches the light beautifully at all hours, and the pace of a water-based safari has a particular and unhurried grace.

Namib Desert — Hoodia Desert Lodge

Day 6 - 8

Day 6 - 8 brings you to Hoodia Desert Lodge, your base for exploring one of Africa's most dramatic desert landscapes. The dunes are best experienced at dawn, when their colours are at their most vivid and the air is still cool. Evenings here — sundowner in hand, the desert turning amber around you — are among the most memorable of any journey.

Sossusvlei — Strand Hotel Swakopmund

Day 8 - 10

Day 8 - 10 places you at Strand Hotel Swakopmund, where the cold Atlantic and the desert edge meet in a landscape like no other. It is a welcome shift in scene, and a beautifully paced pause between wilder chapters.
