Overview

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure. This route is part of our exclusive Namibia luxury safaris packages, taking you from the rolling red dunes of the Kalahari to the wildlife-rich plains of Etosha, stopping by the dramatic coastlines of Swakopmund. Crafted for the independent traveler, this guarantees the perfect blend of freedom, comfort, and luxury.

Itinerary

Day 1-3: Camelthorn Kalahari Lodge

Your adventure begins at Camelthorn Kalahari Lodge, located in the Intu Afrika Kalahari Game Reserve. This lodge offers a unique opportunity to experience the vast Kalahari Desert, with guided game drives and the chance to spot desert wildlife like springbok and oryx. Relax in the comfortable accommodations after a day of exploring the stunning desert landscapes.

Region: Intu Afrika Kalahari Game Reserve

View Lodge →

Day 3-5: Canyon Roadhouse

Next, head to Canyon Roadhouse, located near the breathtaking Fish River Canyon. The rustic charm of this lodge complements the dramatic scenery of one of the world's largest canyons. Spend your days exploring the canyon's awe-inspiring views and the surrounding desert, and unwind at the cozy lodge in the evenings.

Region: Fish River Canyon

View Lodge →

Day 5-7: Alte Villa

Continue your journey to the peaceful Alte Villa in Luderitz, where you'll experience a tranquil stay surrounded by the beauty of the Namibian coast. Explore the town's colonial architecture and the nearby ghost town of Kolmanskop, or simply enjoy the serene environment at the lodge, perfect for relaxation and reflection.

Region: Luderitz / Kolmanskop

View Lodge →

Day 7-8: At Kronenhof Lodge

Your next stop is At Kronenhof Lodge, nestled in the Namib Desert. This luxurious lodge offers stunning views of the desert landscape and a peaceful retreat. Enjoy a guided excursion through the desert, or simply relax in the comfort of the lodge, surrounded by the beauty of the Namibian dunes.

Region: Namib Desert

View Lodge →

Day 8-10: Desert Homestead Lodge

Head to Desert Homestead Lodge near Sossusvlei, where you'll stay amidst the iconic red dunes of the Namib Desert. The lodge offers a perfect blend of luxury and nature, with opportunities to explore the surrounding dunes and enjoy the tranquility of the desert. Experience the awe-inspiring Sossusvlei dunes at sunrise for an unforgettable adventure.

Region: Sossusvlei / Namib Desert

View Lodge →

Day 10-11: The Windhoek Luxury Suites

Conclude your tour in Windhoek at The Windhoek Luxury Suites, offering a relaxing and comfortable end to your journey. This luxurious suite provides easy access to explore the capital city, where you can discover local markets, restaurants, and cultural sites. Enjoy the final moments of your trip in the heart of Namibia before heading home.

Region: Windhoek, Namibia
