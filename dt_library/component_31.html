Day 1

Experiences THE WEINBERG WINDHOEK GONDWANA COLLECTION NAMIBIA • WINDHOEK Day Notes Welcome to Namibia — a land of wide horizons, cultural richness, and striking contrasts. Upon arrival at Hosea Kutako International Airport, you'll either collect your rental vehicle directly at the airport, have it delivered to your accommodation, or collect it from the Windhoek depot — whichever suits your travel preferences best. Enjoy a relaxed night preparing for the journey ahead.

Day 2

Experiences BAGATELLE KALAHARI GAME RANCH • KALAHARI NAMIBIA Day Notes After a relaxed breakfast in Windhoek, the morning unfolds with a private cultural experience and a glimpse of the city’s landmarks. By midday you journey south into the Kalahari, where rolling red dunes and golden savannah welcome you. Settle into Bagatelle Kalahari Game Ranch and end the day with a sundowner drive, glowing desert skies, and dinner beneath a canopy of stars. Included Morning  - Cultural Township Tour  Afternoon - Bagatelle - Combo Sundowner Drive + Cheetah Excursion (Shared activity - Private not possible)

Day 3

Day Notes Begin the day with a guided Bushman walk, where San trackers share timeless knowledge of the Kalahari’s land and traditions. In the afternoon, explore the red dunes and savannahs on a game drive, with the chance to encounter giraffe, kudu, ostrich, and cheetahs. After sunset, an optional night drive reveals the desert’s hidden creatures before you return to the lodge for a candlelit dinner beneath the stars. Included Private Morning Bushman Walk (later departure for a more exclusive experience) 2 Lunch Packs (for the following day) Excluded Optional extra activities not included

Day 4

Experiences LÜDERITZ NEST HOTEL • LUDERITZ Day Notes Today’s journey carries you from the red sands of the Kalahari to the cool Atlantic shores of Lüderitz, one of Namibia’s most scenic drives. Pause at the Quivertree Forest near Keetmanshoop, where surreal aloe trees rise among ancient boulders, before continuing west through shifting desert landscapes. Arriving in Lüderitz, soak up the crisp sea air and the town’s historic charm before settling in for a peaceful night by the ocean. Expert Tips We will make Dinner reservation at the TOP Recommended Seafood Restaurants closer to date.

Day Notes Devote the day to Kolmanskop, Namibia’s iconic ghost town where sand drifts through abandoned homes and time stands still. Begin with a private sunrise session in the golden light, return later for sunset when the ruins glow in soft tones, and spend the hours between at leisure in Lüderitz. It’s a full day steeped in atmosphere, history, and haunting beauty. Day 6

Experiences ANDBEYOND SOSSUSVLEI DESERT LODGE • NAMIBRAND NATURE RESERVE Day Notes After breakfast, leave the Atlantic coast behind and journey inland toward the Namib Desert. Pause at Garub to witness Namibia’s legendary wild desert horses before continuing through vast plains and mountain backdrops. By late afternoon you arrive at the luxurious &Beyond Sossusvlei Desert Lodge, where glass-fronted suites and endless skies set the stage for an unforgettable desert stay.

Day 7

Day Notes Rise early for a soul-stirring excursion to Sossusvlei and Deadvlei, where sunrise paints the world’s highest dunes and ancient trees stand frozen in time. Return by midday to relax at the lodge before choosing from optional desert activities such as nature walks, quad biking, or visiting Sesriem Canyon. As night falls, gather under Namibia’s unspoiled skies for an unforgettable stargazing experience. Included &Beyond Sossusvlei Excursion Additional lodge activities as time permits

Day 8

Experiences STRAND HOTEL SWAKOPMUND • SWAKOPMUND Day Notes Begin before sunrise with a breathtaking hot air balloon flight over the Namib Desert, followed by a champagne breakfast among the dunes. Later, journey north across vast desert plains toward the coast, where cool Atlantic breezes and Swakopmund’s seaside charm await. Settle into the Strand Hotel and end the day with a special seafood dinner overlooking the ocean. Included Hot Air Balloon Flight with Namib Sky Balloon Safaris Champagne Breakfast in the dunes Return of Car Rental

Day 9

Day Notes Set out early on a private full-day excursion to Sandwich Harbour, where the Namib’s soaring dunes plunge into the Atlantic Ocean. Guided by specialists from SandWaves, you’ll explore shifting sands, tidal flats, and wildlife-rich lagoons, pausing often for photography in this surreal landscape. A gourmet picnic among the dunes adds to the experience before returning to Swakopmund for a seaside evening and dinner at Ocean Cellar. Included Private Full-Day Sandwich Harbour Excursion with SandWaves Gourmet Picnic Lunch in the dunes Expert Tips Video Link: Sandwich Harbour https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pOYnCMYY_2E

Day 10

Experiences WILDERNESS SERRA CAFEMA • HARTMANN VALLEY Day Notes After breakfast, transfer to Swakopmund Airport for your private charter flight north. Soar over vast dune seas, rugged escarpments, and winding riverbeds before landing in the remote Kunene region. Here, Serra Cafema Lodge awaits — a sanctuary of quiet luxury set along the Kunene River, where desert silence and starlit skies frame your evening. Included Private Charter Flight from Swakopmund to Serra Cafema

Day 11

Day Notes Spend the day immersed in the wild beauty and cultural depth of Namibia’s far north. With your private guide at Serra Cafema, explore the vast Hartmann Valley and life-giving Kunene River, pausing for a sensitive, authentic visit to a Himba settlement. Depending on your interests, venture into the dunes, take a boat ride, or simply enjoy the wilderness on foot, before ending with sundowners and a fireside dinner under the stars. Included Private Guided Exploration of Hartmann Valley & Kunene River Private Visit to a Himba Settlement (arranged respectfully) Optional lodge-based activities (dune drives, river boat trips, guided walks)

Day 12

Experiences ONGUMA CAMP KALA • ONGUMA NATURE RESERVE Day Notes This morning, depart Serra Cafema by private charter, flying directly to the Onguma Private Nature Reserve for more time in one of Namibia’s most exclusive safari destinations. Take in sweeping aerial views as desert escarpments give way to Etosha’s shimmering pans. At Onguma Camp Kala, unwind in your private suite before setting out on a private afternoon game drive, where abundant wildlife and golden light create unforgettable moments. Included Private Charter Flight from Serra Cafema to Onguma Camp Kala Private Afternoon Game Drive in Onguma or Etosha Optional lodge-based activities (as time and interest permit)

Day 13

Day Notes Spend your final day in Namibia embraced by the exclusivity of Onguma Camp Kala. Begin with a guided morning game drive into Onguma or Etosha, where soft light and abundant wildlife make for memorable sightings. Back at camp, relax by your plunge pool or waterhole before one last golden-hour outing, whether on a drive or from the hide. End the evening with a starlit farewell dinner, surrounded by the wild sounds of the bush. Included Guided Morning Game Drive in Onguma or Etosha Optional lodge-based activities (as time and interest permit)

Day 14

Day Notes After a final sunrise and breakfast at Camp Kala, transfer to the nearby airstrip for your private charter flight to Windhoek. As you ascend over Onguma’s plains, bid farewell to Namibia’s sweeping deserts, wildlife, and timeless cultures. Upon arrival at Hosea Kutako International Airport, connect with your onward international flight, carrying home both memories and the soul of the journey. Included Transfer to Onguma Airstrip Private Charter Flight from Onguma to Windhoek (Hosea Kutako International Airport)
