Best Time to Visit Botswana

Botswana offers incredible wildlife experiences year-round, but the environment changes dramatically with the seasons. Depending on what you want to see, different times of the year offer unique advantages.

The Dry Season

May to October

Considered the best time for traditional wildlife viewing. As water holes dry up, animals congregate in large numbers around permanent water sources like the Chobe River and the Okavango Delta. The weather is generally sunny and dry, though nights can be chilly from June to August. This is also when the Okavango Delta floodwaters typically peak.

The Green Season

November to April

The summer rains transform the arid landscapes into lush, green paradises. This is the calving season for many herbivores, which in turn attracts predators. It's the ultimate time for bird watching as migratory species return. While some areas might become inaccessible due to mud, the dramatic skies and vibrant scenery make it a photographer's dream.
