Day 1

Experiences KALAHARI RED DUNES LODGE • KALAHARI NAMIBIA Day Notes Touch down in Namibia and collect your Car Rental Vehicle at Hosea Kutako Airport, then set off on your first scenic journey into the Kalahari. Arrive at Kalahari Red Dunes Lodge, where elegance meets desert stillness, and settle into your suite. As night falls, savour a refined dinner beneath a canopy of stars — a flawless beginning to your Namibian adventure.

Day 2

Day Notes A full day to embrace the serenity of the Kalahari. Unwind amid sweeping red dunes, explore the lodge’s wild surroundings, or simply bask in the stillness of the desert. In the afternoon, head out on a game drive and end the day with a sundowner as the desert glows in golden light. Included Game Drive Sundowner

Day 3

Experiences HOODIA DESERT LODGE • SOSSUSVLEI Day Notes Journey onward to the edge of the Namib Desert, where the warm hospitality of Hoodia Desert Lodge awaits. After settling in, embrace the desert’s golden hour with a magical sundowner — your first taste of the timeless beauty that defines Namibia. Included Sundowner Experience

Day 4

Day Notes Wake early for Hoodia’s signature Sossusvlei Tour, an unforgettable journey into Namibia’s most iconic desert landscapes. From climbing the towering red dunes to walking across the surreal salt pan of Deadvlei, today is pure desert magic. Included Hoodia Signature Sossusvlei Tour

Day 7

Experiences CAMP KIPWE • TWYFELFONTEIN Day Notes Drive north along Namibia’s Skeleton Coast, stopping en route at Cape Cross to witness one of the world’s largest Cape fur seal colonies. Continue inland into the dramatic landscapes of Damaraland and settle into the welcoming Camp Kipwe, where rugged scenery and desert silence create an unforgettable atmosphere. As evening falls, enjoy dinner with sweeping views of this striking wilderness.

Day 8

Day Notes Spend the day exploring the wonders of Damaraland from your base at Camp Kipwe. Join a guided excursion in search of the elusive desert-adapted elephants, venture out to see the ancient rock engravings at Twyfelfontein, or take a scenic drive through dramatic basalt landscapes. Return to camp to relax by the pool or enjoy the golden light over the Aba Huab valley before dinner under the stars. Included Guided Excursion to track Desert-Adapted Elephants Visit to Twyfelfontein Rock Engravings Optional lodge-based activities (as time and interest permit)

Day 9

Experiences ETOSHA OBERLAND LODGE • ETOSHA SOUTH Day Notes En route to Etosha, make a short stop at the Petrified Forest before continuing to Etosha Oberland Lodge. This luxury retreat on the park’s doorstep combines refined comfort with exceptional wildlife viewing, offering the perfect base for your safari days ahead.

Day 10

Day Notes Your first full day in Etosha is devoted to exploring the park’s famed waterholes and sweeping plains. With luck, you’ll encounter elephants, lions, rhinos, and more on a full-day game drive led by Oberland Lodge. Included Full Day Etosha Game Drive with Oberland Lodge

As evening falls, a gourmet dinner awaits under the Namibian sky.
